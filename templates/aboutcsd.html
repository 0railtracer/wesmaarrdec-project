About the Client

The Client

Our client’s name is Ms. Lucy Felix-Sadiwa. She was appointed in 2022 during the 1st semester as an ICT cluster coordinator of Western Mindanao Agriculture, Aquatic and Natural Resources, Research, and Development Consortium “WESMAARRDEC” and the Department Head of Computer Science. She is also the Technical Consultant of SERDACC.

Ms. Lucy finished her Bachelor’s Degree (BSCS) at Ateneo de Zamboanga University in 1999 and had her Master’s Degree in Computer Science in 2021. She taught at Universidad de Zamboanga from 1999 to 2003 as a Computer Science teacher. Since 2009 until now, she's been a teacher at Western Mindanao State University. WESMAARRDEC website was created last year 2022 in a web builder platform called WIX.
